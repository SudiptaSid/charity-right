Mercy Without Limits distributes food baskets in Ramadan to provide people in need with the means to make something to eat for iftar.

Thousands of meals are brought to struggling families in numerous countries, including Syria, Iraq, India, Jordan, Lebanon, Pakistan, Sierra Leone, and Turkey.

The end of Ramadan is marked by Eid al-Fitr, the Festival of Breaking Fast. We are actively working to help those facing difficulty partake in this celebration that closes the most Holy Month.

You can help - by donating towards a Food Basket ahead of Ramadan, you can relieve families from hunger for the entire month. Just one basket provides enough food for a small family until Eid - or for a larger family for two weeks during Ramadan.

Our choices, their futures

During this time of year families across the world enjoy gathering together to prepare and cook meals ahead of Iftar. This is one of the many comforting aspects to the most Holy Month that many of us look forward to.

Mercy Without Limits is aware that there are families in numerous countries who struggle to find adequate food all year long - and we are taking action to solve this. By bringing people in need food baskets, we can work to ensure they have a steady source of food for the entire month - but we can only do it with your help.
