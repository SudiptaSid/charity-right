Ramadan can be made more rewarding by daily philanthropy

During Ramadan, Muslims are required to fast and perform acts of charity. By fasting from sunrise to sunset, one seeks spiritual cleansing, a closer relationship with God, and a greater understanding of human suffering. Muslims fast to practice self-discipline and sacrifice, as well as reflect on and show compassion for the poor and less fortunate.
