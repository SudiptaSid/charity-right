Use Your Crypto For The Greater Good

It’s official! Charity Right are now accepting cryptocurrency donations, working with our partners at The Giving Block.

Your donations will go towards feeding children in communities that are truly in need across 8 countries: Afghanistan, Yemen, Ethiopia, Sudan, Malawi, Turkey, Pakistan, and Bangladesh.

We believe that education is the long-term solution to bring communities out of poverty. This is why we have one focus: we feed children at school with daily nutritious meals.

Don’t hodl your donations

We understand you may wish to hodl your crypto to see the long-term gains, but we wouldn’t advise you do the same for donations. When you donate to Charity Right, you’re truly making a difference both in the short-term and long-term so don’t hesitate to feed a child today.
