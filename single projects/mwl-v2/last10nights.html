The Last 10 Nights: A Beginning for Hope

Laylat-al-Qadr, or the Night of Power, is the holiest eve on the Islamic calendar. The importance of this night is higher than those associated with 1,000 months of devotion and commemoration to Allah (SWT).

This night falls within Ramadan’s final 10 days, although the exact date is unidentified.

The power of this night combined with the power of mercy presents an unparalleled opportunity to purify our nafs while improving the living conditions of orphans, widows, and the most vulnerable communities worldwide.
