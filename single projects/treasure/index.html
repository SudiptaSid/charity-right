What do I need to do?

First, recruit a team of up to five. Not just any team. A team of heroes. Together, you’ll need to:

  • 1

    Raise the most money
    (at least £3,600 per team)

    If there are five of you, that’s only £720 per person, which you will raise through adedicated fundraising page.

  • 2

    Answer the clues
    correctly

    The accuracy of your answers will count towards your final score. Be attentive and think out all your answers.

  • 3

    And reach the finish
    line in the fastest time

    Those who raise the most money, correctly answer the clues, and reach the finish line in the fastest time, will win the treasure hunt.

Sign up today

Sign up today by registering your team to secure your space. You’ll receive a welcome email, then all the details you need via email closer to the date of the treasure hunt.

In the meantime, our team will be on hand to help you if you need anything. Places are limited, so please don’t delay.
