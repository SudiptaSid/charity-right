About Food Friday

Around the world, millions of children are trapped in poverty.

This is because of a lack of nutrition, inadequate sanitation and poor health and wellbeing that deeply affects life chances.

In addition, these children are often excluded from education or can’t go to school because they need to work or do chores at home.

Meals in schools can start to transform lives forever

By providing school meals, we offer parents and guardians a huge incentive to send their children to school.

As a result, these children are much more likely to stay healthy, get a life-changing education and simply have fun with friends.
