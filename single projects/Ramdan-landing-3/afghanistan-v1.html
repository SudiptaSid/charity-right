The Daily School Meals Programme

The Ideal Solution To Alleviating Hunger AND Educating Children At The Same Time…

Education is the best defence against poverty because it opens the doors to many different opportunities in the future.

However, the pandemic has forced many schools to close, while the current climate has left parents no choice but to push their kids into work, or worse, get their daughters married early.

We believe children should be given the chance to help themselves out of poverty.

That’s why our Daily School Meals Programme provides children with hot meals as an incentive to get children back into school. Not only can children get an education in a safe environment, it’s the best chance they have of getting out of poverty permanently.
