Islamic Relief’s Dedication to Distributing Zakat

According to Islamic guidelines, Islamic Relief collects and distributes zakat to those in need. According to the Holy Qur’an (9:60), there are eight categories of people who qualify to receive zakat:

  • The Poor
  • The Needy
  • The Collectors of Zakat
  • Captives
  • Travelers
  • Those Burdened with Debt
  • In The Cause Of Allah (SWT)
  • Those Whose Hearts Are to Be Won Over

Researchers have generally agreed that collectors of zakat are among the eight groups entitled to receive zakat. As such, IRUSA is eligible to use up to 12.5% of donations made to zakat-specific funds for core work, including administrative and operational functions.

Many prefer to give zakat al-Mal during Ramadan, but the option to give is always open. However, when it comes to zakat al-Fitr, it should be given before Eid prayer.
