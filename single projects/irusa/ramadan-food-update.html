Food Boxes for Ramadan

In the spirit of Ramadan, Islamic Relief USA, a nonprofit charity that provides humanitarian aid to more than 50 countries, will continue assembling food boxes for families in need.

DONATE

What impact will your Donations have?

Muslims throughout the world observe Ramadan as a time of fasting and spiritual reflection. However, it also serves as a reminder that a significant number of people are suffering from hunger on a daily basis.

Islamic Relief USA packages food boxes that hold approximately 30 pounds of food each. This will feed a family of five to seven for one month.

Food is purchased locally whenever possible, and boxes generally contain rice, wheat, lentils, oil, canned meat, and dates.

Support IRUSA’s efforts towards Food Aid

We work tirelessly so that people never have to live in fear of food insecurity. Food aid makes up the largest part of Islamic Relief USA's humanitarian efforts.

To provide healthy food to those in need, we have a wide range of programs, including worldwide distributions during Ramadan and Qurbani, mobilizations in response to natural disasters, and food pantries of quality food.

Across the globe, we’re working hard to make sure that our neighbors have enough to eat.
