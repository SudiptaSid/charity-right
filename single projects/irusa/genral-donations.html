The Power of Food

Muslims throughout the world observe Ramadan as a time of fasting and spiritual reflection. However, it also serves as a reminder that a significant number of people are suffering from hunger on a daily basis.

Islamic Relief USA packages food boxes that hold approximately 30 pounds of food each. This will feed a family of five to seven for one month.

Food is purchased locally whenever possible, and boxes generally contain rice, wheat, lentils, oil, canned meat, and dates.

Zakat can end global Poverty

Zakat is one of the five powerful pillars of Islam, along with prayer, fasting, pilgrimage (Hajj), and belief in Allah (SWT) and His Messenger Prophet Muhammad (SAW).

Muslims who own wealth over a certain amount - known as nisab - are required to pay zakat, which is 2.5% of their wealth.

In the Holy Qur’an (2:110), we read, “And establish prayer and give zakat, and whatever good you put forward for yourselves—you will find it with Allah; surely Allah sees what you do.”

We must fulfill the promise of Zakat in order to help those in need. When you give your zakat through Islamic Relief USA, it's powerful, impactful, and its potential multiplies.
