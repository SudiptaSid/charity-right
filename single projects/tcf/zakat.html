Promote faithfulness and success

Zakat is one of the most effective ways to help those in need while following Allah's wishes (SWT). Throughout the Holy Qur'an, zakat is mentioned

Zakat contributes to creating a more equal, balanced society, while allowing those who have been blessed with good fortune to support their less fortunate brothers and sisters.

Your Zakat:
  • Provides decent living conditions to the poor and the needy by establishing a self-supporting Ummah.
  • The community's impoverished and needy members can be relieved of their suffering so that they can live independently.
  • Removes selfishness and greed from the heart of the giver.
  • Reduces the gap between the poor and the rich/promotes equality.
  • Harmonious coexistence within the community.
  • Fosters a healthy relationship between the rich and the less fortunate.
  • Promotes social responsibility.
  • The poor and needy are freed from jealousy and envy.
  • Reduces poverty.
  • Cleanses the giver's wealth.
  • The recipient feels a sense of security and belonging to the Ummah.
  • Giving a feeling of satisfaction to those who expect that their wealth will be blessed by Allah.
