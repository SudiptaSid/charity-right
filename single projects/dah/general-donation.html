How Will Your Donations Help this Ramadan?

Community service: When we take care of the most vulnerable in the community, Allah aids us when we feel helpless. We have the opportunity to begin with a clean slate this Ramadan.

Youth: Your contributions will go towards education, training, and counseling for the youth. Your donations have an impact for generations.

Women’s Programs: Help us support women’s constructive contribution. community engagement, and leadership; to promote acceptance, unity through diversity, and cohesion amongst the women of Dar Al-Hijrah this Ramadan.

Ramadan Iftar: From the ibadah of providing food for people arise many other acts of worship, such as creating comradery, love, and hope among the community.

Food Bank: By donating just $1 daily per month, you can provide Iftar and Suhoor to at least one person. Even if 7 people sign up to donate $1 daily, we can provide Iftar to 30 people.

What more can you do This Ramadan?

Zakat: In Islam, people are obligated to hold their wealth in trust for Allah, since everything belongs to Allah. In this way, we purify our possessions by setting aside a portion for community members who need our help. Giving zakat during Ramadan, the holiest month, multiplies the rewards.

Sadaqah: Sadaqah purifies the heart, soul, and wealth of the giver while uplifting the community.

Masjid Renovations: Inclusivity is our goal this Ramadan. Will you help us accommodate more community members by supporting renovations for generations?
