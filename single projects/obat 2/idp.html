How do we help internally displaced communities?

As a 501(C)(3), non-profit organization based in the United States, OBAT Helpers provides support, education, and economic empowerment to displaced and stranded populations in camps in Bangladesh.

OBAT's programs empower thousands of people. Each year, over 150,000 people have access to clean water and sanitation facilities.

A variety of educational programs are available to almost 10,000 students. OBAT's health facilities treat almost 7,000 people annually. OBAT's Women Empowerment Centers provide sewing training to close to 300 women. The Family Assistance and Qurbani Programs benefit over 70,000 people annually.

In seeing the young employees of OBAT Helpers, many of whom are displaced themselves, you can witness humanity at work with beautiful and contagious smiles, a can-do attitude, and a sense of purpose and belonging.

They are doing amazing and inspiring work in the toughest and most heartbreaking of situations. Join them in any way you can this Ramadan and make a lasting difference.
