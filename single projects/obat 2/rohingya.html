OBAT's Rohingya Relief Program has undertaken the following projects since it began operations in 2017:

Current Projects:
  • 13 learning centers for almost 900 kids
  • 2 Health Centers. 62,381 patients treated in 2021 alone. Includes maternity and dental section, triage unit and a fully stocked pharmacy
  • Almost 1,300 beneficiaries of the Women Empowerment Center and Income Generation Programs.
  • Relief and Family Assistance Program. Over 35,000 beneficiaries of distributions of food rations, infant care and hygiene kits, educational materials and qurbani meat.
Completed Projects:
  • Five mobile medical camps
  • 270 shelters built for Extremely Vulnerable Individuals
  • Playground for children
  • 82 tube wells for providing clean water
  • 1,504 shelters were built to provide families with secure and safe homes for the cyclone season
  • Distribution of shelter kits, solar lights and meals

Why help the Rohingya?
