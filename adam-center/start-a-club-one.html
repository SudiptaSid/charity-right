Start a New Club at ADAMS Center

Thank you for your desire to start a Club at ADAMS. Clubs are a great way to get active at your Masjid and to connect with others through recreational and special interest activities here at ADAMS. In order to ensure the success and safety of your Club, the following guidelines should be followed to quickly get your Club approved, promoted and operational.

Start a Club at ADAMS Center in three simple steps.

Register Here Donation & Payment

Three Simple Steps for Starting your ADAMS Club

Download the Club Application Packet

Download the Club Application Packet and read the Policy Guidelines for ADAMS Clubs.

Complete the Club Proposal Form

Complete the Club Proposal Form (Fillable PDF), then submit to the ADAMS Clubs Coordinator (clubs@adamscenter.org)

Sign

Sign the Club Advisor Responsibilities Form and attach it to your Club Proposal. Include your Club's leadership team that will be responsible for all aspects of your Club, and also who will be your Club Advisor.

All Club applications are reviewed on a weekly basis for approval by the ADAMS Clubs Coordinator (clubs@adamscenter.org).
