JUMA’H

Over the past three decades, the ADAMS Center has grown into one of the largest Muslim communities in the Washington, D.C. area. Today, we serve the areas of Sterling, Ashburn, Centerville, Chantilly, Gainesville, Great Falls, Herndon, Leesburg, McLean, and Reston, in northern Virginia. Our community continues to grow each year as more families join us.

Masjid Rules & Regulations

  • Bring your wudu

  • Bring your prayer mat

  • Children must remain close to parents

  • Wear a mask at all times

  • Maintain 6 ft distance

Please do not come to the masjid

if you have any of the following symptoms

  • 01 Fever or chills
  • 02 Headache
  • 03 Sore throat
  • 04 Diarrhea
  • 05 Fatigue
  • 06 Muscle or body pain
  • 07 Recent loss of taste or smell
  • 08 Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • 09 Congestion or runny nose
  • 10 Cough
