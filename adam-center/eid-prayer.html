To keep all who attend ADAMS Center and its branches safe, healthy, and in compliance with the rules and regulations with state, local and CDC guidelines with highest safety measures, we have temporarily adopted these rules effective immediately. Masjid Rules & Regulations

  • Bring your wudu

  • Bring your prayer mat

  • Children must remain close to parents

  • Wear a mask at all times

  • Maintain 6 ft distance

Please do not come to the masjid

if you have any of the following symptoms

  • 01 Fever or chills
  • 02 Headache
  • 03 Sore throat
  • 04 Diarrhea
  • 05 Fatigue
  • 06 Muscle or body pain
  • 07 Recent loss of taste or smell
  • 08 Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • 09 Congestion or runny nose
  • 10 Cough
