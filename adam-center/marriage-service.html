Annual Healthy Families Retreat

Our vision for the Healthy Families Retreat is to create a day at the masjid for you to reconnect with yourself, your family, and your community thru programs lead by Imam Magid ,Imam Abdur'rafa and trained professionals. Reinvigorate yourself with spiritual exercises, create new bonds of friendship, and participate in a day of strengthening the community. "Every strong community is born of the solidity of families and individuals, become a part of the beauty of this connection."
