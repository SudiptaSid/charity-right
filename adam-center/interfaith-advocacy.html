Interfaith

Over the past three decades, the ADAMS Center has grown into one of the largest Muslim communities in the Washington, D.C. area. Today, we serve the areas of Sterling, Ashburn, Centerville, Chantilly, Gainesville, Great Falls, Herndon, Leesburg, McLean, and Reston, in northern Virginia. Our community continues to grow each year as more families join us.

ADAMS is committed to serving the interfaith community.

Over the years, the ADAMS Center has cemented their relations with prominent interfaith organizations. We diligently promote dialogue with individuals with diverse belief practices, which makes us more active in developing positive relationships with our neighbors and community. We have been continuously supportive of many churches, synagogues, and temples throughout the Washington, D.C. area.

Notably, the ADAMS Center is a member of many interfaith associations including:

01

Cornerstones (formerly named Reston Interfaith) – http://www.cornerstonesva.org

02

Fairfax County Faith Communities in Action – http://www.fairfaxcounty.gov/ncs/cic/cic-fcia.htm

03

Interfaith Conference of Metropolitan Washington – http://www.ifcmw.org
