New Muslim Support Group

“Unquestionably, by the remembrance of Allah hearts are assured”
- Quran 13:28

The ADAMS New Muslim Support Group is available to provide education and resources to new Muslims and individuals interested in Islam. Please contact us for any specific requests or questions at learn_islam@adamscenter.org.

Islam 101
Series

Islam 101 series is open to anyone who is interested in basic Islamic knowledge. This class takes place at 7pm every Thursday, all year long. The curriculum is built to allow students to join at any time, and there are no fees or registration necessary. Classes are held online with Zoom. Email learn_islam@adamscenter.org for more information and for the link to attend class online.

Learn to Read
Quran

Learn to Read Quran is a class available to adults interested in learning the basics of how to read the Quran. The class is primarily focused on assisting new Muslims with learning to read Quranic Arabic. These classes run on a five month schedule, online with Zoom on Thursdays at 6pm. Please email learn_islam@adamscenter.org for start dates and changes to the schedule. The online link will be provided when the new sessions start.

Want to join NMSG's Buddy Program? Please email learn_islam@adamscenter.org for more info.
