Taleem ul-Quran

ADAMS’ Taleem ul-Qur’an program started in 1997 with one teacher and 10 students. MashAllah, it now has an enrollment of 200 students, and a staff of 11 teachers, many of whom are former ADAMS Taleem ul-Qur’an students. Our main focus is on teaching students four and above how to read the Qur’an correctly, the way that it was revealed to our beloved Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).

ADAMS Sterling Tahfeedh Ul-Quran

The Tahfeedh ul Quran Program provides instruction in an Islamic environment, under the guidance of qualified and caring staff. Focus is primarily on:

  • Ensuring students have the essential tools to attain Quality Quran Memorization
  • Learning the correct pronunciation of letters, tajweed rules in theory & application.
  • Increasing knowledge and application of the fundamentals of Islam – 5 pillars of Islam.
  • Increasing knowledge and application of ahadith and sunnah.
  • Increasing understanding of meanings of the surahs
  • Increasing knowledge of Quranic Arabic,
  • Developing a strong connection and love for the Quran.
  • Helping students transform knowledge into rightful actions using a wholisitc approach and a positive learning environment.
  • Developing discipline and citizenship to be responsible and contributing members at the community level and society at large.
