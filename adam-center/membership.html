Membership

Over the past three decades, the ADAMS Center has grown into one of the largest Muslim communities in the Washington, D.C. area. Today, we serve the areas of Sterling, Ashburn, Centerville, Chantilly, Gainesville, Great Falls, Herndon, Leesburg, McLean, and Reston, in northern Virginia. Our community continues to grow each year as more families join us.

Assalmu Alaykum Warahmatullah Wabarakatuh, Dear Community,

ADAMS is pleased to announce our Membership Program. As part of our updated plan, you will be entitled to receive benefits and discounts for a variety of services.

These services include benefits such as:

  • Exclusive membership rates and access to ADAMS educational programs -members will be able to register and sign up for schools' programs for both children and adults.

  • Discounts when reserving various ADAMS facilities for events - members can receive up to 10% on discounted fees for renting facilities on ADAMS properties.

  • Nomination and voting privileges for Executive Committee and Board positions - members can nominate other members from the community and vote for Executive Committee and Board positions.

  • Opportunity to speak at Board Meetings - members will be able to attend and speak at various board meetings throughout the year.

  • Receive priority for officiating marriages - members will be given priority for scheduling and planning for marriages with our marriage coordinator.

If you are a current paying member of our membership program, then InshAllah you will receive a membership card with your name. To receive and enjoy these discounts, please note that you will have to show an ID along with your ADAMS membership card at time of purchase. Also, please see list of participating business sponsors on our website for discounts. Visit our website at www.adamscenter.org for complete information on our various programs and activities.

If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us at (703) 433-1325 x114 or e-mail at membership@adamscenter.org. May Allah bless you and our community with best regards!
