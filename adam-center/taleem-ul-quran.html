Taleem ul-Quran

ADAMS’ Taleem ul-Qur’an program started in 1997 with one teacher and 10 students. MashAllah, it now has an enrollment of 200 students, and a staff of 11 teachers, many of whom are former ADAMS Taleem ul-Qur’an students. Our main focus is on teaching students four and above how to read the Qur’an correctly, the way that it was revealed to our beloved Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).

Register Registation Guidlines

Students begin their journey with Noorani Qaidah classes that are held in the evenings, Monday through Thursday. Students, who can already read, continue to perfect their Tajweed during the weeknights, or they attend the weekend classes to start memorizing and further perfecting their Tajweed. Those with strong motivation are chosen by teachers to attend special Qur’an teacher training classes, in which they are trained over years to become Qur’an teachers themselves. We pray that Allah (swt) accepts our efforts and continues to allow this program to serve the Qur’an and the community.
