One of the largest Muslim Communities in Washington, D.C

Join us in our mission to provide religious, social, and educational services to enable the Muslim community to fulfill its responsibilities and contribute to the betterment of society by embodying and exemplifying leadership and excellence and fostering peace.

Download the app to advance your spiritual journey and give back to the community.

ADAMS’ vision is to be a model American Muslim community with which Allah (God) is pleased.

We offer Islamic education for all ages, from children, to youth, to adults. The goal is to instill in them the Islamic values of morality, compassion, service to humanity and to prepare them to become positive contributors to society as American Muslims.

Learn how you can increase your Islamic educational knowledge and how to assist others on the same journey.

Why choose ADAMS Center?

Our vision is to have a compassionate and well-educated community living in dignity and self-sufficiency.

Masjid constructions

Whether it’s $10 or $20, contributing to build a masjid secures an entire house in Jannah for the giver. Donate today.

Zakat

All our programs are zakat-eligible. We are dedicated to distributing funds to those most in need.

Sadaqah

We offer community iftars food, groceries nourishment, and financial aid to community members (in need or otherwise) so that they can celebrate Ramadan and sustain themselves during the holy month.

Community services

Therapy programs, support groups, an outlet for every need in regard to financial assistance and spiritual wellbeing and mental and physical health programs in an atmosphere of compassion.

Sponsor an Iftar

We serve daily Iftar to 300-400 community members and Suhur in the last 10 days of Ramadan.

ADAMS Clinic

We ensure every uninsured, low-income individual has access to primary care and preventative services. We provide care regardless of county and state lines.
