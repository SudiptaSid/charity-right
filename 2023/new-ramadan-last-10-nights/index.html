Mission

To develop American communities with sacred values through programs that revive, connect, grow, and transform all.

Vision

Jannah Circle works to transform the culture of how diverse families and individuals of all ages are supported in a community through programs and resources for their physical well being, parenting, socialization, mental health, access to expert education, and self-development workshops. Our purpose is to inspire and mobilize a community that can then be empowered to help even more causes and create long-lasting social change.

American Muslims are struggling. 15-25% report anxiety disorders, 9-30% report mood disorders, and over 50% report one or more major problems with their marriage (The FYI).

Our organization has been tirelessly working to provide essential support and services to women and children since 2018, through spiritual and social events and experiences.
