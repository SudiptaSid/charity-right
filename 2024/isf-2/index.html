Amplifying the Voices of American Muslims

At ISF, we are committed to amplifying the voices of American Muslims across various platforms, aligning with the tenets of justice:

  • Empowering Filmmakers to Inspire:

    Through our film initiatives, we empower talented filmmakers to tell our stories, providing authentic representation and amplifying diverse voices in media.

  • Combating Disinformation with Honest Journalism:

    ISF scholars in journalism uphold the principle of truth, challenging false narratives about Islam and Muslims in America.

  • Bringing the Muslim Voice to Media:

    We support initiatives that ensure truthful and accurate representation of American Muslims in media, aligning with the theme of truth during Ramadan.

  • Wielding the Power of Policy:

    ISF's public policy programs focus on promoting equity and human rights, preparing American Muslims to advocate for just policies that benefit all members of society.

  • Cultivating Strong Leaders:

    By empowering passionate individuals, ISF cultivates leaders who champion justice and equity within their communities, embodying the spirit of Ramadan.

  • Elevating Elected Officials:

    ISF contributes to the elevation of American Muslims in public office, ensuring representation that advocates for justice and fairness for all.

  • Improving Public Opinion:

    Through increased representation in media, ISF combats stereotypes and promotes understanding, fostering a more just and inclusive society.

  • Increasing Representation in Law:

    ISF encourages American Muslims to pursue careersin public interest law, focusing on human rights and social justice advocacy.

  • Promoting the Power of the Pen:

    ISF scholars publish works that explore issues of justice and equity, contributing to the discourse on social change and progress.

  • Challenging the Status Quo:

    ISF alumni are change agents who challenge unjust systems and policies, working towards a society founded on the principles of justice and equality.

Donate to ISF today and help us continue to empower the future leaders of our community. Together, let's uphold the tenets of justice and make this Ramadan a time of meaningful action and positive change.
