Let’s Change Lives Together

BDesh Foundation aims to alleviate poverty, improve health and sanitation, and support education initiatives in Bangladesh and beyond. With a keen focus on empowering communities, BDesh addresses the needs of crisis zones and assists Rohingya Refugees through comprehensive projects.

What can we achieve this year?

Last Ramadan, the BDesh Foundation successfully implemented numerous tube wells, healthcare initiatives, and educational programs. This year, we're aiming even higher. With your support, we can exceed our past achievements and extend our reach to assist additional communities in dire need.

Your contribution can significantly impact the lives of people in Bangladesh. Support our holistic efforts in eye care, healthcare, tubewell, sanitation, Ramadan Food Baskets, and child sponsorship. Join BDesh and sponsors to fund long-term community aid programs.

Let's amplify our impact together.
