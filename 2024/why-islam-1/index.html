What’s Happening in Gaza?

Since October 7th, Gaza has portrayed the beauty of Islam simply by enduring. Their outward reliance on Allah, their constant display of gratitude amidst devastating loss, and their unwavering refusal to leave their native land have won the hearts of millions around the world. 

From picking up the Quran for the first time to reaching out to learn more about Islam, people from around the globe are sharing their journey to the truth because their hearts have been affected by the plight of the Gazans.

How YOU can help?

By supporting WhyIslam’s quest to deliver accessible, easy-to-understand knowledge, you can nurture the hearts eager to learn about our deen. Send your zakat, sadaqah, or a general donation to WhyIslam to continue spreading the message of Islam.

Gaza is a testament that the beauty of our deen is apparent to those sincerely seeking it. Let’s cultivate the seeds of truth growing in the hearts of the people by making Dawah material accessible today.
