Dedicated to ending hunger in Bangladesh and the Rohingya Refugee Camps, BDesh aims to help as many people as possible. With your support, we can feed families and bring joy and happiness to communities. 

What can we achieve this year?

Last Ramadan, we reached many families in need and helped them with our food baskets. As we reflect on it, we face the dilemma of the Ramadan food crisis. The situation is dire still, and without immediate action, many will break fast this month with unbearable hunger and hopelessness. 

With your support, BDesh aims to feed even more families and spread hope this Ramadan. 

Why Food Baskets with BDesh?

Time is running out! With each passing day, more and more families face the prospect of welcoming Iftar without proper food or clean drinking water. This Ramadan, let’s unite to resolve this crisis head-on. 

Your prompt response or $50 donation can support BDesh to turn the tide and ensure no one breaks fast hungry. BDesh food baskets aren’t just meals; they are a glimmer of hope in the darkness of hunger.
