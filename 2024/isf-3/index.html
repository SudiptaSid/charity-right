Embrace Ramadan

This Ramadan, ISF is working to bolster the tenets of justice across the four influential areas where we support promising American Muslim students, public interest law, public policy, journalism and film.

In 2023 alone ISF awarded over $3.06 Million, granting scholarships, supporting filmmakers, placing interns, and recognizing alums as trailblazers. Our alums have achieved incredible things from working on the Capitol to creating films that tell the stories of Muslims that would have otherwise been untold.

As we embrace the reflection, giving and community spirit of this blessed month. ISF invites you to join on this journey and make this Ramadan one we’ll never forget through an act of enduring generosity: sponsoring a scholarship for American Muslim students.
