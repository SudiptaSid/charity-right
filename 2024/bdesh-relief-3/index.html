Dedicated to ending the suffering the Rohingya women and children face, BDesh is currently running 3 projects on the ground for aid. 

What can we achieve this year?
BDesh has been providing life-supporting aid to the Rohingya Muslims in the camps. Moreover, our Project Women Care is dedicated to training women for independence in the Women Empowerment Centers. 
Plus, orphaned and destitute children will be selected for care in the Shobuj Polly Child Care Centers. Let’s AIM Even Higher This Ramadan! Your immediate support will make all this, and more possible!

Why Ramadan Rohingya Relief with BDesh?
Your immediate support is a lifeline for Rohingya women and children. After the displacement, and the fire at the largest Rohingya Refugee camp, most have lost all their worldly possessions.
