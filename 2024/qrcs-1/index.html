Empowering Lives Across the Globe

The Qatar Red Crescent Society is committed to fulfilling the humanitarian goals of the Qatar National Vision 2030. With the help of our 29,000 volunteers, we have provided over 2.1 billion Qatari Riyals in aid for the last 5 years, benefiting more than 53 million people.

Through our campaign in 2024, this Ramadan, we’re focused on providing clean water and adequate nutrition to those in need, especially in Qatar, and extending our efforts beyond to support the people of Palestine and 17 other countries.
