Stand Together, Heal Gaza, Rebuild Lives this Ramadan

As Ramadan approaches, the people of Gaza face an unprecedented crisis. The recent Israeli airstrikes have left countless lives shattered, with families torn apart and communities in ruins. The need for immediate assistance is dire, and we cannot stand idly by while our brothers and sisters suffer.

Why Your Help is Critical

The situation in Gaza is nothing short of a humanitarian catastrophe. Thousands of innocent civilians have been injured, many facing the prospect of permanent disability without urgent medical intervention. Families are struggling to find food, water, and shelter amidst the devastation.

Building Gaza's Medical Infrastructure

In collaboration with the Ministry of Health, we are working tirelessly to strengthen Gaza's medical infrastructure. Through initiatives like the Shifaa, Nasser, and European projects, we are helping to rebuild hospitals and clinics, ensuring that vital medical services are accessible to all.

How You Can Make a Difference

Your support is urgently needed to help us continue our lifesaving work in Gaza. By donating today, you can provide critical medical care to those who need it most. With price points ranging from $100 to $5000, every contribution makes a difference.

Providing Lifesaving Medical Care

At Fajr Scientific, we are committed to making a difference in the lives of those affected by this crisis. Through our Gaza Campaign - Ramadan, we are mobilizing resources to deliver essential aid to the Palestinian community. Our focus is on providing critical medical care to those in need, particularly through targeted surgeries.

Targeted Surgeries for Adults & Children

Our medical teams are performing a range of surgeries to address the urgent needs of both adults and children affected by the crisis. From orthopedic and plastic surgeries to vascular and cardiothoracic procedures, every operation has the potential to transform lives and restore hope.

Join Us in Making a Difference

This Ramadan, let us come together as a global community to stand with the people of Gaza. Your generosity has the power to heal wounds, rebuild lives, and bring hope to those who need it most. Together, we can make a difference.
