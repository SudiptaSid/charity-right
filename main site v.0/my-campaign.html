Our story

I am raising money to provide emergency food packs for the people of Yemen.

66% of Yemenis cannot afford food. 2 in 3 people are at danger of dying from hunger.

Since 2011, Yemen has been suffering. From what began as a ‘revolution’ has now become a humanitarian disaster. In the past 9 years Yemen has gone through a series of conflicts which have destroyed cities, caused the death of thousands and has left millions vulnerable.

We can’t let this continue for another decade.

What we do

Charity Right is aiming to provide families impacted by war, famine and now Covid19 with family food packs containing essential food items.

The Emergency Food Packs contain:

  • 10kg Rice
  • 15kg Flour
  • 2kg Lentils
  • 4.8kg Kidney Beans
  • 5kg Sugar
  • 5L Oil
  • 2kg Dates

*For families with infants we will also provide baby milk
