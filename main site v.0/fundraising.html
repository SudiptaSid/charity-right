How you can support our life-changing work

Create an appeal

Creating a fundraising appeal about an issue you care about is easy. All you have to do is set up a Charity Right account and follow the simple steps.

After that, it’s all about raising awareness of what you’re doing and maybe organising some fundraising events to help get the money rolling in.

Celebrate a life

Giving in memory is a truly special way to remember a loved one and transform the lives of some of the world’s most vulnerable people.

Your gift, and the gifts of family and friends, will fund food that gives children and adults hopeof escaping poverty and transforming their lives.

This year, why not do something extra special to celebrate your birthday?
