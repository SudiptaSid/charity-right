Transforming lives with the power of food

We develop and deliver projects so they have the greatest impact possible on the lives of some of the world’s poorest and most vulnerable people.

School meals

Our regular, nutritious meals are an effective incentive for impoverished parents to send their children to school, instead of making them work or do domestic chores at home.

Donate View

Pregnancy packs

Malnutrition during pregnancy and in the two years following birth can cause irreversible damage to a mother and their child. That’s why our pregnancy food packs are so vital.

Donate View

Quran programme

We provide hot meals to Quran students, many of which are orphans. This regular nutrition is key to giving them the strength to remain focused throughout their studies.

Donate View

Family food packs

In hard-to-reach places, such as refugee camps and remote villages, we deliver food packs to families who don’t know where their next meal is coming from.

Donate View

Emergency appeals

With our supporters’ help, we respond swiftly and effectively to disasters around the world to ensure vulnerable children and adults receive life-saving food.
